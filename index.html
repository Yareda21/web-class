    • Celebrate Culture, Tradition, and Unity

    The Ethiopian New Year, also known as "Gena," is a time of joy, gratitude, and cultural heritage. Join us in celebrating this vibrant occasion with traditional festivities, music, and community spirit. Explore the beauty of our traditions, the warmth of our people, and the significance of this special day.
